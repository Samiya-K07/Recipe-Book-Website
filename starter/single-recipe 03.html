Best-Ever Hot Chocolate

My Best-Ever Hot Chocolate is made on pure chocolateâ€“not cocoa powder and dried milk but rich bittersweet chocolate and creamy milk. You know when you drink a thick, rich hot chocolate and wonder how they make it? This is that recipe. The secret is REAL dark chocolate. This Hot Chocolate has so much depth and richness from lovely dark chocolate. Make my rich and decadent Best Ever Hot Chocolate recipe and warm the hearts of your family and friends during the holiday season.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cup (8 oz/240g) bittersweet chocolate (72% cocoa solids)
  • 3 1/2 cups (28floz/840ml) milk
  • 1 cup (8floz/240ml) pouring cream
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract, optional

Instructions

  1. In a heavy bottomed saucepan, bring the cream, milk, and chocolate to a simmer.
  2. As it starts to bubble, whisk it and watch it thicken.
  3. Pull it off the heat.
  4. If you would like to add vanilla extract, you can do that now.
  5. Taste. If you would like it a little sweeter, add in some white sugar or your choice of alternative.
  6. Serve hot with frozen whipped cream or marshmallows.
